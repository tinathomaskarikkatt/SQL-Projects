# 🍽️ Customer Purchase Behavior Analysis using SQL Server

A SQL-based customer analytics project that explores purchasing behavior, product performance, and customer loyalty using data from an online food ordering platform. The project analyzes customer transactions, product sales, and Gold Membership performance to generate actionable business insights that can improve revenue, customer retention, and marketing strategies.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Understanding customer behavior is essential for improving customer satisfaction and increasing business revenue. This project analyzes customer purchase data from an online food delivery platform using SQL Server.

The analysis focuses on identifying purchasing trends, customer preferences, product popularity, and the impact of Gold Membership on customer spending. By answering business-driven questions with SQL, the project demonstrates how database analysis can support strategic decision-making.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze customer purchase behavior.
- Identify the highest and lowest revenue-generating products.
- Evaluate customer purchasing patterns.
- Compare Gold Members and Non-Gold Members.
- Study yearly sales trends.
- Generate business recommendations using SQL analysis.

---

# 📂 Dataset Information

The project is based on an online food ordering platform and consists of **5 relational tables**.

### Database Tables

- **Sales** – Customer orders
- **Product** – Menu items and prices
- **Users** – Customer signup information
- **User_Name** – Customer names and IDs
- **Goldusers_Signup** – Gold Membership details

The database is designed using **Primary Keys** and **Foreign Keys** to maintain relationships between tables.

# 📊 Business Questions Explored

The analysis answers several business-focused questions, including:

- Which product generates the highest revenue?
- Which products contribute the most overall sales?
- Which product is purchased most frequently?
- How much revenue comes from Gold Members vs Non-Gold Members?
- Which products are preferred by Gold Members?
- How do customer purchasing patterns change after joining Gold Membership?
- What are the yearly revenue trends?
- What are customers' first purchased items?
- Which products encourage repeat purchases?

---

# 📈 Key Findings

- **Butter Chicken** generated the highest revenue (**₹1700**).
- **Shahi Paneer** was the most frequently purchased item.
- **Mango Lassi** generated the lowest revenue.
- Gold Members contributed **55% of total revenue**, despite representing only half of the customers.
- Gold Members placed more frequent orders than Non-Gold Members.
- **Dal Makhani** was the most common first purchase for new customers.
- Revenue peaked in **2017** before declining slightly in subsequent years.
- Customers consistently showed loyalty toward a few popular dishes, creating opportunities for personalized recommendations.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the SQL analysis:

- Promote high-performing products such as Butter Chicken, Shahi Paneer, and Mutton Biryani.
- Improve visibility or reassess low-performing menu items.
- Expand the Gold Membership program to increase customer lifetime value.
- Recommend customers' favorite dishes to encourage repeat purchases.
- Introduce promotional offers for first-time customers using popular dishes.
- Re-engage inactive customers with targeted discounts and loyalty campaigns.
